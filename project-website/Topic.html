Negative Impact of AI in Schools

Introduction

The integration of Artificial Intelligence in education promises efficiency and innovation, but it also poses significant challenges that must not be overlooked.

Key Issues

  • Academic Integrity: AI tools such as ChatGPT can be misused by students to complete assignments dishonestly.
  • Data Privacy: AI systems often collect and process sensitive student information, raising concerns about security and consent.
  • Bias and Fairness: Algorithms may reflect societal biases, affecting assessments and recommendations for students unfairly.
  • Teacher Displacement: Over-reliance on AI could undermine the role of human educators, leading to reduced job opportunities and mentorship quality.

Academic References

  1. Smith, J. (2023). Ethics of AI in Education. Journal of EdTech, 12(3), 45–60.
  2. Brown, T. & Lee, R. (2022). AI and Academic Dishonesty. Educational Review, 58(1), 22–34.
  3. Nguyen, A. (2023). Student Data Privacy in the Age of AI. EdTech Policy Journal, 17(2), 88–102.

Conclusion

While AI can enhance learning, careful policies and ethical considerations are essential to mitigate its negative effects on schools.
